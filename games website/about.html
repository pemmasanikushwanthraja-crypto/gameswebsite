About Simple Home

Simple Home is a small, opinionated starter template focused on speed, accessibility and ease-of-use. It uses pure HTML/CSS and lightweight SVG icons so you can drop it into any project and customise quickly.

Mission

Provide a clear, maintainable starting point for personal sites, portfolios, and small product pages — without heavy frameworks or build steps.

Quick facts
  • No frameworks
  • Accessible nav
  • Easy to customise
